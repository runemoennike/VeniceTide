Keep your feet dry: Never travel to beautiful Venice without this app!

Venice Tide shows you the current tide level in Venice, Italy, as well as predictions for the next 2-3 days. Add the locations you visit most often and the app will show you if these locations are flooded or not, and if they will be flooded in the near future. 

Simple and easy to use. Useful for citizens of Venice as well as tourists. Includes live tile that lets you see important information easily.
